NeurIPS 2025 Social Event

The Role of AI in Scientific Peer Review

Join us for an interactive discussion on shaping the future of scientific peer review with AI. This social brings together researchers, practitioners, and stakeholders to explore actionable solutions for a more efficient, fair, and transparent review system.
